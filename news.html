THE PRECIOUS GEMS UPDATE

Posted: December 23th, 2021 at 4:45 PM

I am happy to announce that I have been working on the next update for the modification. The mod will be on gemstones and some bugfixes for 2.0.0. I will be adding 7 new crystal like models that can generate rare and some common minerals.

They can be found in caves and both in the nether and surface. The location will be dependant on the type of gemstone its for. Glowstone and Quartz will be found in the nether.

Diamonds and redstone will be found at or below Y level 20 in surface caves. Emeralds and Lapis can be found at or below Y level 40 in surface caves. Amethyst can be found at or below Y level 50 in surface caves.
